# AI Ops Playbooks

This repository captures workflows, systems, and experiments around using AI in client servicing, publisher monetization, and operational scaling.

## What this includes

- Publisher monetization strategies and frameworks  
- AI-led workflows for campaign execution and optimization  
- Internal systems for tracking, reporting, and team coordination  
- Process playbooks for scaling client servicing operations  

## Why this exists

Most operational knowledge in adtech and client servicing lives in silos. This repository is an attempt to document and structure those learnings—especially where AI can meaningfully improve speed, clarity, and outcomes.

## Current focus areas

- Building repeatable AI workflows for campaign and content execution  
- Improving visibility through dashboards and structured tracking systems  
- Experimenting with AI tools to reduce manual effort across teams

This is a living repository and will continue to evolve with new experiments, frameworks, and learnings.
